About Us

Motif art and design academy was established in the year 2010, by Mr. Kailash Pansare. It is among the best art schools in Maharashtra India. Motif academy provides knowledge of various types of art and design. The most important salient feature of this institute is that is no age block and it offers an opportunity to develop personnel in various professions. We main focus is towards providing personalized attention and best education to our students. Also we aim to create a friendly and comfortable environment to work in, which helps our students to excel in their work. Motif academy’s objective is to educate artist and people toward success and self-satisfaction towards their careers and hobbies.

Motifs distinct features

1)Industry oriented syllabus. 2)Sufficient duration specially designed according to courses. 3)Time flexibility 4)Age flexibility. 5)Profession flexibility. 6)Comfortable, friendly and encouraging environment. 7)Certificate courses 8)Finest teaching flow. 9)Student projects as per current industry requirements. 10)Work exposure to gain practical knowledge. 11)Development of individual Student portfolio. 12)Indoor and outdoor study according to course. 13)Expert guidance. 14)Job Assistance.
